## 💫 Prompt to paste into Lovable

**Project Name:** BEZEN-EDU | The Inner Teacher Journey

**Goal:**
Create a gentle, interactive MVP (web + mobile) that guides trainee teachers through a 5-minute daily emotional awareness journey.
The experience combines mindfulness, breathing, and compassionate thinking, helping teachers strengthen inner calm and professional presence.

---

### 🧩 ENTITIES

**1. User**
Represents each trainee teacher.
Fields:

* name (string)
* email (string, optional)
* value_focus (enum: Compassion, Listening, Responsibility, Calmness)
* created_at (datetime)

---

**2. Journey**
Represents the user’s daily emotional session.
Fields:

* user_id (relation to User)
* date (datetime)
* mind_text (string)
* mind_reframe (string)
* heart_emotion (enum: Fear, Hope, Frustration, Curiosity)
* body_state (integer, 1–5 slider)
* action_step (string)
* soul_belief (string)
* calm_score (integer, 1–5)

---

**3. Card**
Represents the final consciousness affirmation card created at the end of each session.
Fields:

* user_id (relation to User)
* text (string)
* value_tag (string)
* created_at (datetime)

---

### 📱 SCREENS

#### 1️⃣ Welcome Screen

**Title:** “Welcome 🌿 Let’s take a short journey of breathing and awareness.”
Elements:

* Input field: name
* Dropdown: Select guiding value (Compassion / Listening / Responsibility / Calmness)
* Button: “Start the Journey”
* Background animation: soft breathing pulse (circle expanding gently)

---

#### 2️⃣ The Journey (Five Stages)

**Stage 1 – BEZEN-MIND**
Prompt: “What thought arises when you think about your teaching today?”
→ Text input.
After submission, display: “Try to see yourself as learning, not failing.”

**Stage 2 – BEZEN-HEART**
Prompt: “Which emotion is present right now?”
→ Choose from Fear / Hope / Frustration / Curiosity.
After selection: show short breathing guide (4-4-4 breathing).

**Stage 3 – BEZEN-BODY**
Prompt: “Notice your body. How calm do you feel right now?”
→ Slider 1–5 (color from red to green).

**Stage 4 – BEZEN-ACTION**
Prompt: “What small action could support you tomorrow in class?”
→ Short text field (example: ‘I’ll listen before I react.’).

**Stage 5 – BEZEN-SOUL**
Prompt: “Write a belief that strengthens your sense of purpose as a teacher.”
→ Text field (example: ‘Every student carries light within.’).

---

#### 3️⃣ Final Screen – Consciousness Card

Display the new affirmation card:

> “I am a teacher who teaches from inner calm 💫.”
> Buttons:

* “Save Card” (creates new Card entity)
* “Start a New Journey Tomorrow”
  Soft fade-out animation + gentle chime sound.

---

### 🎨 DESIGN STYLE

* Background color: `#F9FBFA`
* Text color: `#225C56` (soft green-blue)
* Font: *Noto Sans* or *Assistant*
* Minimal, calm layout — plenty of white space, rounded buttons, breathing animations.

---

### 💡 FEATURES

* Simple login (first name only)
* Auto-save the latest Journey
* “My Cards” section to view past affirmations
* Basic dashboard for mentors: number of journeys completed per week

---

### 🛡️ PRIVACY & ETHICS

* Collect only first name and optional email.
* No sensitive or medical data.
* Purpose: personal emotional empowerment, not diagnosis or therapy.
* Show clear “Safe Space” disclaimer.

---

---

### ✨ FINAL MESSAGE (last screen)

> “Thank you for giving yourself this moment.
> Remember — calmness isn’t a place you arrive at,
> it’s a way of walking through life.”

### 🧠 Optional Components for MVP+
